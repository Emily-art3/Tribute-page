Tupac Shakur

The Rose That Grew from Concrete

Tupac Amaru Shakur, also known as 2Pac, was one of the most influential figures in music and culture. Born in 1971, Tupac became an iconic rapper, actor, and social activist. His music spoke about the struggles of urban life, systemic oppression, and social injustices, making him a voice for those who were often unheard. Even after his tragic death in 1996, his impact has continued to resonate with generations, keeping his legacy alive.
