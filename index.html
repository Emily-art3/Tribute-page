Mr.Tom Mboya

A Prominent Kenyan Politician

Tom Mboya was a prominent Kenyan politician and an influential figure in the country's struggle for independence. Born on August 15, 1930, in the rural village of Kendu Bay, Mboya became known for his visionary leadership and dedication to social justice.

Key Contributions:

  1. Education Advocate : Mboya was a strong proponent of education, establishing the East African Scholarship Scheme.
  2. Political Leadership: As a member of the Kenya African National Union (KANU), Mboya played a crucial role in the fight for independence.
  3. Pan Africanims: Mboya was a staunch advocate for Pan-African unity, believing in the importance of solidarity among African nations.
  4. Tragic Legacy: Mboya's life was cut short when he was assassinated on July 5, 1969, a tragic event that shocked the nation and led to widespread unrest.
